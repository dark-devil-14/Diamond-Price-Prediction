# 📊 Diamond Price Prediction Visualizations

To avoid confusion, the visualization plots generated during the analysis, feature engineering, modeling, and evaluation phases have been organized into dedicated categories.

---

## 📂 1. Data Analysis (`images/1_data_analysis`)
Focuses on Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to understand the features and target variable.
*   **`Categorical_feature_analysis.png`**: Boxplots analyzing the price distributions across categories for `cut`, `color`, and `clarity`. Shows that higher clarity and color grades command higher median prices, but also illustrates overlaps.
*   **`Correlation_Heatmap.png`**: A Pearson correlation heatmap of all numeric features. Highlights high multicollinearity between the physical dimensions (`x`, `y`, `z`) and `carat`, pointing to the need for feature selection/regularization.
*   **`Price_vs_Carat.png`**: A scatter plot of price vs. carat weight. Clearly shows the exponential curve of price increases, particularly noting massive price jumps at round carat thresholds (e.g., exactly 1.0ct, 1.5ct, 2.0ct).

---

## 📂 2. Feature Engineering (`images/2_feature_engineering`)
Visualizations related to feature transformations, selection, and data leakage checks.
*   **`feature_engineering.png`**: Visual comparison of raw features against newly engineered columns (like `volume` ($x \times y \times z$) and `carat_density`).
*   **`lasso_feature_selection.png`**: Coefficient path plots showing how L1 (Lasso) regularization drives weaker weights to absolute zero as the penalty parameter ($\alpha$) increases.
*   **`lasso_best_features.png`**: Bar chart showing the final non-zero coefficients selected by the Lasso model, identifying the most predictive features.
*   **`data_leakage_demo.png`**: Diagnostics illustrating the hazard of scaling before splitting, validating that our preprocessing pipeline is clean of target/data leakage.

---

## 📂 3. Model Performance (`images/3_model_performance`)
Evaluation of regression predictions across various algorithms.
*   **`Linear_Regression_model.png`**: Actual vs. Predicted scatter plot for the baseline Linear Regression model, showing its struggle with non-linear relationships.
*   **`AdaBoost_Regressor_model.png`**, **`Gradient_Boosting_Model.png`**, **`XGBoost_model.png`**: Diagnostic plots for ensemble methods. They display tighter fit patterns around the identity line, demonstrating significantly better performance than linear baselines.
*   **`Model_Comparison.png`**: Combined bar chart showing a comparison of RMSE and R² metrics across basic algorithms.
*   **`grand_leaderboard.png`**: The master leaderboard sorting all tested models (including tuned versions) by test R² and RMSE, highlighting the best performer.

---

## 📂 4. Validation and Diagnostics (`images/4_validation_diagnostics`)
Overfitting and stability diagnostics.
*   **`actual_vs_predicted_bias_variance.png`**: A prediction interval/error plot highlighting the variance of residual errors across different price ranges.
*   **`bias_variance_comparison.png`**: Comparison chart indicating the training vs. testing error trade-off.
*   **`learning_curves.png`**: Training vs. Validation loss plotted over training set sizes. Used to verify whether the model would benefit from more training samples or if it has reached its capacity limit.
*   **`cross_validation_stability.png`**: Boxplot showing the spread of R² scores across 5 cross-validation folds for the primary model, verifying that performance is stable across different subsets of data.
*   **`cv_model_comparison.png`**: Comparison of cross-validated R² means and standard deviations, showing stability indicators (Green = Stable, Orange = Moderate, Red = Unstable).
*   **`regularization_comparison.png`**: Performance comparison of regularization estimators (Lasso, Ridge, ElasticNet) against Ordinary Least Squares (OLS).

---

## 📂 5. Hyperparameter Tuning (`images/5_hyperparameter_tuning`)
Optimization paths and validation curves.
*   **`hyperparameter_tuning_results.png`**: Performance metrics plotted across the search space grid to visualize the optimal region.
*   **`optuna_tuning_comparison.png`**: Optimization history chart generated by Optuna, showing the path of the objective function (CV R²) over 20 search trials.
*   **`rf_overfitting_depth.png`**: Validation curve for Random Forest max depth, indicating the exact point where training score improves but testing score drops (signaling the onset of overfitting).
*   **`rf_tuning_comparison.png`**: Bar chart comparing default, manually tuned, and Optuna-tuned tree regressor metrics.
